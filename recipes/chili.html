Description

Awesome beef chili winner of multiple back to back workplace Chili Cookoffs.

This chili is super simple to make and the perfect meal for crisp autumn days.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pkg Williams Original Chili Seasoning Seasons 4 lbs meat
  • 4 lbs Ground Beef
  • 2 (14.5 oz) Diced Tomatoes. I use zesty chili seasoned tomatoes
  • 2 cans Ranch Style beans with jalepenos
  • 2 cans Ranch Style beans
  • 2 (8 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • 1 Onion
  • Nature's Seasoning to taste
  • Cayenne Pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Brown 4lbs of ground beef with diced onion, minced garlic, & Nature's Seasoning to taste.
  2. Drain the grease from browned beef & place into a 6qt Crock-Pot.
  3. Mix in beans, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, & chili seasoning.
  4. Simmer on low for 4-5 hours adding cayenne pepper to taste.
